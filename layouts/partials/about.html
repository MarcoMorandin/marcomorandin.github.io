About Me

I am a student at the University of Trento, currently pursuing a degree in Artificial Intelligence Systems. My academic journey began with a bachelor’s degree in Software Engineering, where I developed a strong foundation in software development, system design, and problem-solving. My passion for technology and its potential to drive innovation led me to specialize in artificial intelligence, a field that fascinates me with its ability to transform industries and solve complex problems.

Beyond academics, I have a variety of interests that shape who I am. I am an avid hiker, always eager to explore new trails and challenge myself in nature. Hiking not only fuels my love for adventure but also provides me with a sense of clarity and resilience. Another passion of mine is origami making, which allows me to express creativity through intricate designs and precise craftsmanship. Cooking is also something I deeply enjoy—it’s a way for me to experiment, learn, and share moments with others.

Collaboration is something I value highly. I am comfortable working in teams, as I believe that the best ideas and solutions often come from collective effort and diverse perspectives. Whether in academic projects, or personal initiatives, I enjoy contributing to a shared goal, learning from others, and taking on challenges together.
